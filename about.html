Our History

Maxi started in 2000 as a simple script to automate the business's "awkward reminder" emails. It worked so well that clients actually *thanked* them for the professional follow-ups. We knew we were onto something. Today, Maxi has grown into a simple, powerful tool dedicated to one thing: helping small businesses manage their cash flow and build better client relationships.
